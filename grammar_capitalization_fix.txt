Update the grammar correction system with this one change:

Remove capitalization from grammar error detection entirely.

Do NOT flag or correct any of these as errors:
- Sentences not starting with capital letter ("hi james, i was wondering...")
- Proper nouns in lowercase ("sarah", "koratrade", "japan")
- "i" in lowercase instead of "I"
- Any other capitalization issues

The grammar correction system should ONLY flag clear grammatical errors such as:
- Wrong verb tense ("I have went" → "I have gone")
- Subject-verb disagreement ("she don't know" → "she doesn't know")
- Wrong adjective/adverb form ("I am very boring" → "I am very bored")
- Missing articles where clearly wrong ("I went to store" → "I went to the store")
- Clearly wrong word form ("I am very tired from the works" → "from the work")

Reason: This is a messaging/chat context. Native English speakers regularly skip capitalization in casual messages and texts. Flagging this would be misleading and unhelpful for learners.
